MS 1 Final - Bias Slot Machine

Domain

Human bias in life.

1 How bias shapes our ways of thinking and how it influences the person who we have bias on, both in reality and in AI world.

2 How to use design to educate people to act without bias in a light and playful way, and what’s the impact of bias education.

Concept Description

Based on facial recognition, the result of a slot machine is no longer defined by luck, but by the operator’s gender, racial, and other outside appearances. The user does not know the mechanism of the slot machine, but certain people who always win and others who never earn anything. And when they figure out why they will understand that’s exactly the mechanism of prejudice in life, without fairness, without principle, some people get preferences. This slot machine aims to raise awareness toward the issue of bias, and to some extent to guide people to act without bias.

Prototypes

Digital version. This version should be made by JavaScript, trough an Open Source API.


Physical version. This version should be made by JavaScript and Arduino, for the API part is JS, and for the physical part is Arduino.


How it works

DEMO1

The most important part of the Slot machine is facial recognition. I tried to use Microsoft Azure Face API, tired to connect it with the webcam on my computer. When I press the button, my webcam can take a picture of my face and send it to Azure to get the result.

For the aesthetical part, I made a Javascript Slot machine. It seems a regular slot machine, and when the button gets pressed, it begins to operate.
