5D - Interaction

The place we choose is High Line park. High Line park is a place with a combination of natural elements, industrial heritage and New York City street views.

Original idea
Use mirrors or mirror papers to make some reflection. When people work through or pass the mirrors, they can feel the combination of city view and nature senary from the imageries in the mirror.

Some preferences

Our first prototype
We brought acrylic mirrors and some silver reflecting paper and went to Highline part to test the places suitable to set up.

During this process, we fond out silver refecting paper is the perfect match for nature and city, because it has a water-like feature, and in this park, there is little element about water. Also, this paper is the sliver, so it has a kind of industrial style. Therefore we decided to use this kind of paper combine the element of water to do something fit the Highline park.

Then we came up with two ideas, one of which was to make a wavy sculpture, where the reflection of plants could be seen on the sculpture as people walked in from a distance. The other is to flatten the water, simulating small pools on the ground, running down the stairs to see how people react.

After finally decided what to do, we began to choose places suitable for our project and did some measurement work. These pictures are the places we chose.
